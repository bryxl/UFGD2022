Abigail Altieri

Abigail Altieri is a graphic designer with a heart for organic forms, experimental typography, and high contrast. Combining playful visuals with research, concept, and community involvement is the core of her design approach. Her work is largely influenced by her interests in sustainability, music, social equity, and photography. In the future, she hopes to positively impact her community through designing systems that are equitable and sustainable.

Arlie Birket

Arlie is a multidisciplinary artist, designer, & music lover. She is excited by projects that are hands-on, require problem-solving, and loves print & layout design. Arlie also has a love for film photography and is often inspired by a sense of nostalgia. Her main goal through design is to experiment, learn new processes and techniques to create each unique outcome. Arlie hopes to work in the entertainment industry and/or collaborate with other creatives!

Brianne' De Los Santos

Brianne’ De Los Santos is a Filipino-Canadian designer and illustrator who specializes in designing for screens. She believes in creating high-quality, research-based user experiences that prioritize accessibility without sacrificing creativity. In addition to UX/UI, she is interested in brand identities, illustration, and animation. After graduating, she plans to move to Atlanta, GA, to pursue a career in experience design.

Christine Reynolds

Christine is a graphic designer, illustrator, and comics enthusiast. Her specializations include packaging, illustration, and editorial design. In all her work, Christine strives to incorporate fun illustrations and quirky characters for a totally unique outcome. She believes that personality is just as important to a product as good design, so she works to imbue a sense of playfulness and creativity in each and every project.

Delaney Dietrick

Delaney Dietrick is a graphic designer and illustrator from Orlando, FL with a love for all things kitschy, weird, and eclectic.
